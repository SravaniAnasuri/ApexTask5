Strength Training

Build muscle and increase your strength with these exercises.

Benefits of Strength Training

Strength training helps you build muscle mass, boost metabolism, and improve bone health. It is essential for overall fitness.

Sample Strength Training Exercises

  • Squats (3 sets of 15 reps)
  • Push-ups (3 sets of 12 reps)
  • Dumbbell Deadlifts (3 sets of 10 reps)
  • Bench Press (3 sets of 8 reps)
